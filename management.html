Student Management System Details

It was one of my first Java projects. The project was designed to mimic the student registration system, and practice Object Oriented Programming, application of authentication layer, simple operations on given databases and put it all together into a logically working pieces. As shown in the pictures:

Students can view avliable courses, register for the favorite ones, and check their current grades.

Processors can retrive information about offered courses registration statistics, and check students who signed up for their courses.

Admin is the most privelaged user in the system. It has access to modify enrollment in the courses, as well as add/ delete students, professors and courses.
